Grow your brand

Followall is a tool that lets you ask users for social media engagement in exchange for more exclusive content. It's a great way to grow your brand and figure out which of your users wish to engage more deeply with the content you are producing.

How does it work?

Get your users to engage with your social media presence.

When growing your brand you'll find your self producing content and encouraging your users to engage with it. A good way to do this is rewarding that interaction.

How does it work?

  1. Select what to put behind "the wall" it could be a link, a video, anything.
  2. Choose from different social networks and social media actions, follows, likes, reshares, etc.
  3. Followall will provide you with a simple plugin and link to integrate with your content.
  4. Your users will engage you in social media and gain access to the content
